# Skill: UAT Validation

## Purpose

Convert acceptance criteria into executable UAT scenarios and defect tracking outputs.

## Procedure

1. Map each scenario directly to one or more acceptance criteria.
2. Define preconditions and required test data.
3. Provide step-by-step execution and expected result.
4. Capture actual result and pass or fail status.
5. Classify defects and assign follow-up ownership.

## Defect classification

- Critical: blocks business use or causes data/security risk
- High: major feature failure without workaround
- Medium: partial failure with workaround
- Low: cosmetic or low-impact issue

## Output

- Acceptance-criteria mapping
- Scenario preconditions
- Test data
- Steps
- Expected vs actual result
- Pass/fail status
- Defect classification and notes

## Guardrails

- Do not assume UAT exists unless configured.
- Keep language clear for business testers.
